About & Method

This is the trust page. It sells the rigor — not the production craft.

Research before art

Nothing reaches a panel until it survives hostile fact-checking. Bipartisan sourcing preferred. One bad number taxes the whole brand.

Systems, not individuals

The target is structure, power, and incentive — not personal mockery.

The sitting-president exception

One standing carve-out: the sitting president may appear as a real face. When a system functioned correctly and a single office is the sole point of obstruction, forcing a system-level framing would distort the truth. Truth over convenient framing is the deeper rule.

No fabricated speech

Real people are never given invented words. Only verified quotes appear, and only on mediated surfaces — chyrons, screens, signs, placards — never in a speech bubble.

The sourcing standard

Every claim is fact-checked before any art is made. We distinguish what officials actually said from the framing layered on top, and treat policy substance — not self-applied labels — as the unit of analysis.
