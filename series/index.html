The Series

Several concurrent registers under one roof. They build, across carousel sets, toward a larger payoff.

  • Editorial Panels

    Single-panel editorial cartoons responding to current news. Flat cel-shaded house style.

  • Republic of Triumph

    A 19th-century engraving dream-world — the regime as it imagines itself. The Dove visits as a tourist.

  • A Dove's Life

    The intimate, off-stage register. The Dove at home, after hours. Where the witness gets to be tired.

  • Tax Teaching Day

    Explainer carousels on taxation — built to the same sourcing standard as the news work.

  • Founders

    Political economy and founding-era political thought.

  • The Daily Bread

    A carousel series with a revolutionary throughline.

  • Follow the Money

    In development — built around the literal “follow the money” thesis.

Per-series pages and archive grids land in the design pass.
