The Dove

The project's protagonist and throughline: a pale off-white dove with a bandaged wing, perpetually trailing an IV pole whose drip bag reads “PEACE ON EARTH.” The line always runs to the Dove — the peace is on life support, and the Dove is the patient.

Dignified, never a clown. Tired, wounded, deadpan, composed. It witnesses; it rarely performs.

Three registers

  • On-stage — the default editorial register.
  • Realm-of-Reality tourist — the Dove inside the dream-world of Republic of Triumph.
  • A Dove's Life — the intimate, off-stage register, where the witness gets to be tired.
